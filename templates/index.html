Who We Are

We are two high school students who feel passionate about an even playing field for all students academically.

error_outline

The Problem

Many students in our school cheat, far more than a couple. Oftentimes this cheating occurs through sharing of test answers between classes or copying off classmates during the test.

lightbulb_outline

The Solution

Our simple web app, Test Flipper, shuffles up the order of test questions as well as the order of answer choices, making cheating nearly impossible between students.

Our Mission

We seek to drastically reduce cheating in schools, since it is unfair to kids who don't have such an advantage. Use of our product is both easy and will reduce cheating significantly.
